### Analysis 1

After doing an exploratory data analysis, it is clear that hosts with multiple listings (i.e. owning multiple properties) that are available
for rent for over 6 monhts per year are dominating in Barcelona. Additionally, the amount of people with a HUTB license for tourist apartments
is minimal, however it must be noted that there is a lot of missing data in this dataset when it comes to license information. Also, just looking at the number of
unique hosts to total listings makes it clear that there is an imbalance in people actually using Airbnb to occasionally rent out rooms compared to those who use 
it as a business.

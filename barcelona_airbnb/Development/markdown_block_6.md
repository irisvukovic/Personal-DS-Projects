# Overall Findings

Comparing total listings to unique hosts (19,410 vs. 6,620), it is clear that a few multi-property owners and companies are 
dominating the Airbnb market in the city. Also, people renting out entire homes and renting out places that are available for 
over half the year are prevalent.

Additionally, only 17.2% of listings are actually casual, with the rest being commercial and medium-term, meaning 
spaces are being (most probably illegally) rented to tourists and short-term visitors all year round through Airbnb, 
taking homes and rooms off of the market for people living there permanently.

Finally, out of 49 neighborhoods analyzed, Poblenou area (80.9) is in the highest scoring HPI neighborhoods, as well as 
l’Eixample (71) and Vilapicina i la Torre Llobeta (76), with the average HPI being 60.5.
